CASCADE Active Learning Evolution Loop

From metagenomic hits to predicted Cas13-like suicide switches. PXDesign designs linkers only (REC + HEPN frozen). Evolution selects the single best variant per generation; we compute all scores with Protenix. Specificity via 1-, 2-, 3-mismatch off-target tests (activity at higher mismatch penalized harder).
