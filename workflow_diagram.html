CASCADE Active Learning Evolution Loop

From metagenomic hits to predicted Cas13-like suicide switches. Evolution selects the single best variant per generation, uses it as the next baseline, and scores specificity via 1-, 2-, 3-mismatch off-target tests (activity at higher mismatch penalized harder).
